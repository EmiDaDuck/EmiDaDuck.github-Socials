Emoji Encoder Unicode Variation Selectors

Hide text invisibly in a carrier emoji or character. Uses the 16 variation selectors U+FE00…U+FE0F to encode UTF‑8 bytes (two nibbles each).

Encode

Note: Some apps normalize/remove variation selectors (e.g. certain word processors). They are most reliable in plain text fields or chat apps.

Decode

Tip: You can paste entire sentences – the decoder will just read all variation selectors found.
